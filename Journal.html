In our mountainous homeland, it would be guilt not to seize the opportunity to gaze upon the majestic peaks that define our landscape. Thus, to etch a lasting memory of the mountains into our minds, we embarked on the Mardi Trek, easy accessibility, and breathtaking views.

Janakpurdham: a place once known only through books, now witnessed firsthand. This city, revered as the birthplace of Sita, offers a vivid glimpse into the rich cultural and historical tapestry of Nepal. Cleanliness of the place stands as an utmost priority, reflceting its respect for both heritage and visitors.
